Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

CPMSD, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information in connection with our property management operations and SMS notification service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Phone number: Provided during system setup to receive SMS notifications.
  • SMS opt-in/opt-out status: Records of consent and opt-out requests.
  • Property management data: Booking schedules, cleaner assignments, and operational summaries used to generate notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending automated SMS notifications related to property management operations, including cleaner confirmations, schedule alerts, completion updates, and weekly summaries.
  • Processing opt-in and opt-out requests.
  • Maintaining records of consent as required by applicable regulations.

4. SMS Messaging Program

By opting in to our SMS notification program, you consent to receive automated text messages from CPMSD, LLC regarding property management operations. Key details:

  • Message frequency: Up to 10 messages per week.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt out: Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance, or email rob@cpmsd.com.

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your phone number or SMS opt-in data with any third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Your phone number and consent information are used exclusively for delivering property management notifications as described in this policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share information only with:

  • Service providers: Third-party platforms (e.g., Twilio) that facilitate SMS delivery on our behalf, subject to their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain your phone number and opt-in records for as long as you remain opted in to our SMS program, and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at rob@cpmsd.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
