About This SMS Program

CPMSD, LLC operates an automated SMS notification system for property management operations. Messages include:

  • Cleaner schedule confirmations and assignments
  • Cleaning completion alerts
  • Escalation notices (e.g., missed cleanings or scheduling conflicts)
  • Weekly operational summaries (revenue, costs, upcoming bookings)
