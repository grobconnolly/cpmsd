CPMSD, LLC — SMS Notifications

Automated property management alerts

About This SMS Program

CPMSD, LLC operates an automated SMS notification system for internal property management operations. Messages include:

  • Cleaner schedule confirmations and assignments
  • Cleaning completion alerts
  • Escalation notices (e.g., missed cleanings or scheduling conflicts)
  • Weekly operational summaries (revenue, costs, upcoming bookings)

How to Opt In

To receive SMS notifications from this program, the property owner provides their phone number during system setup and activates notifications. By providing your phone number and enabling notifications, you consent to receive automated text messages from CPMSD, LLC related to property management operations.

You may also opt in by texting START to the designated number.

Message Frequency & Rates

You will receive up to 10 messages per week, depending on booking activity.

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by texting STOP to the number from which you received messages. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed.

For help, text HELP or email rob@cpmsd.com.
